Some other reasons why you should choose an electric lawn mower:

Does not need fossil fuels to run, so there will be no co2 emitted. Which means less emissions overall. Requires less maintenance, so you will be saving money on buying parts and money for service. No need to pay for gas or other kinds of fuel, so you are saving money over time. Is quieter than its fossil fuel powered counterparts. Lightweight, which makes it easier to store and transport.
